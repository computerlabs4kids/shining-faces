Computer Labs for Kids

Computer Labs for Kids was founded in 2009 by Shira Sandford.

We are a 501c3 organization that attempts to provide technology to children in need.
Some of our programs have been in Israel, India, Portugal, and in many cities in the USA.
We believe that technology can be used to help improve children's lives and gain needed life skills.
